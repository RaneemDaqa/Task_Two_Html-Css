Arrabeh town

Arrabeh is a beautiful town in the Jenin Governorate of the northern West Bank. It is surrounded by rolling hills and fertile farmland, and is known for its rich history and culture. The town has traditional Palestinian buildings with stone houses and narrow, winding streets that reflect its long history. One of the special features of Arrabeh is its historic palaces, especially the Abed Al Hady's Palaces. These grand buildings, with their detailed designs, highlight the wealth and influence of the families who built them. The palaces are a source of pride for our community and attract visitors who want to learn about Arrabeh's history and culture.
